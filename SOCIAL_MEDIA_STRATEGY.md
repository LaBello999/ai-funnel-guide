# AI Funnel Guide – Social Media & Content Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the Instagram and TikTok content strategy for **AI Funnel Guide**, including content pillars, posting schedules, KI-automation workflows, and growth tactics.

## Platform Strategy

### Instagram (@aifunnelguide)

**Target Audience**: Entrepreneurs, digital marketers, affiliate marketers (25-45 years old)

**Content Mix**:
- 40% Educational content (tool tips, funnel strategies)
- 30% Comparison content (GetResponse vs KlickTipp, etc.)
- 20% Behind-the-scenes / Case studies
- 10% Community engagement (polls, Q&As)

**Posting Schedule**: 3-4 posts per week + 5-7 Stories daily

**Engagement Goals**:
- Grow followers to 10K in 6 months
- 5-8% engagement rate
- Drive traffic to website via link in bio

### TikTok (@aifunnelguide)

**Target Audience**: Younger entrepreneurs, Gen Z side-hustlers (18-35 years old)

**Content Mix**:
- 40% Quick tips (30-60 second funnel hacks)
- 30% Tool reviews (short, punchy comparisons)
- 20% Trending sounds + funnel content
- 10% Viral challenges (affiliate marketing themed)

**Posting Schedule**: 1-2 videos daily (consistency is key)

**Engagement Goals**:
- Reach 50K followers in 6 months
- 10-15% engagement rate
- Drive traffic to website via link in bio

## Growth Tactics

### Instagram Growth

1. **Hashtag Strategy**: Use 20-30 relevant hashtags (mix of sizes)
2. **Engagement**: Like/comment on 50+ posts in niche daily
3. **Collaborations**: Partner with micro-influencers (10K-100K followers)
4. **Reels**: Post 1-2 Reels weekly (algorithm favors video)
5. **Stories**: Post daily Stories with stickers (polls, questions)
6. **Link in Bio**: Use link aggregator (Linktree) to drive traffic

### TikTok Growth

1. **Consistency**: Post 1-2 videos daily (algorithm rewards consistency)
2. **Trending Sounds**: Use trending audio in 50% of videos
3. **Hook First**: Grab attention in first 3 seconds
4. **Hashtags**: Use 3-5 relevant + 1 trending hashtag
5. **Engagement**: Respond to comments within first hour
6. **Duets & Stitches**: Engage with other creators' content
7. **Series**: Create recurring series (e.g., "Tool Tuesday", "Funnel Friday")

## Conversion Optimization

### Instagram to Website

- **Bio Link**: Use Linktree with CTAs (Free Guide, Comparisons, Tools)
- **Link Stickers**: Add clickable links in Stories (requires 10K followers)
- **Swipe-Up Stories**: Once you reach 10K followers
- **CTA in Captions**: "Link in bio" with specific CTAs

### TikTok to Website

- **Bio Link**: Direct to homepage or specific landing page
- **Video CTAs**: "Link in bio" at end of videos
- **Engagement**: Reply to comments with link suggestions
- **Collaboration**: Partner with creators who link to your site
